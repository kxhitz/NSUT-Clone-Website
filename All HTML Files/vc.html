Prof. Jai Prakash Saini Prof.

Jai Prakash Saini is the Founder Vice-Chancellor of Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT), New Delhi. He joined NSUT as first Vice-Chancellor on 26 September, 2018. Professor Saini holds B.Tech in Electronics Engineering in 1987 from KNIT Sultanpur; M.Tech. in Electrical Engineering in 1996 from IIT Kanpur; accomplished Ph.D. in Electronics Engineering from KNIT Sultanpur from Dr. R.M.L. Avadh University, Faizabad in January 2001 and has been a passionate teacher.

Prof. Saini joined as the Director of erstwhile Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology, New Delhi on 25th July, 2017 till 25th September 2018. Before joining NSIT, he was working as Director, Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Handicapped U. P. Kanpur for two terms i.e. from July, 2009 to January, 2010 and from November, 2015 to July, 2017; and Principal of Madan Mohan Malviya Engineering College, Gorakhpur from Jan. 2010 to Dec. 2013; simultaneously he was holding the additional charge of Principal in MKR Engineering College of information Technology, Azamgargh (U. P.). Prof. Saini is a Professor of Electronics & Communication Engineering in Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering & Technology (BIET), Jhansi.

Prof. Saini possesses more than 31 years of experience in academics, researches and academic administration. He was coordinator in U. P. State Entrance Exam for two terms in 2008 and 2015 and also he coordinated SEE on-line counseling from 2004-08. He was Additional Examination Controller from January, 2001 to July 2004. He served well for various responsibilities in BIET Jhansi i.e. Head of the Department for three tenures; Dean Research and Development; and associated with world bank assisted TEQIP project.

Prof. Saini has served as member of academic and research committees of various Universities. Besides serving as member of several Governing Bodies of Government Institutions and Universities like Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar Institute of Information Technology Bijnore; Manyawar Kanshi Ram Engineering College of Information Technology Azamghar; Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar Institute of Information Technology Banda; Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Handicapped, UP Kanpur; Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering & Technology Jhansi; Harcourt Butler Technological Institute Kanpur; National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology; Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College (MMMEC), Gorakhpur and Ministry of Communication & Information Technology, Govt. of India; Utter Pradesh Technical University, Lucknow; GGSIP University, New Delhi.

Prof. Saini has published more than 200 International / National research papers and Supervised a number of Ph.D. & M. Tech, thesis. He is reviewer in various international journals. His research contributions in the area of Electronic Engineering appeared in the international journals and various conference proceedings published by Springer-Verlag, Elsevier and IEEE. He is life member of ISTE and fellow member in IETE. He has long standing academic interests in semiconductors, digital transmission and in mobile communication. He had delivered many lectures / keynote address and chaired various technical sessions during nation and international conferences and training programme. He also organized and contributed to several faculty development programs for teachers and training programs for student and officers of the government organizations.
